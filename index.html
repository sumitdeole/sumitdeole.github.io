I am a research assistant at Trier University, Germany, where I work with Prof. Dr. Marc Oliver Rieger. I graduated with a B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from VJTI, Mumbai, India in 2008. After that, I began my journey in the beautiful world of data science, focusing on applied economics. I have been fortunate to work with a fantastic set of industry experts and researchers in the past. I have Ph.D. degree in Economics from the University of Halle-Wittenberg, Germany where I was advised by Wolf-Heimo Grieben. During my doctoral studies, I was introduced to the state-of-the-art data science methods of causal inference. The research subsequently conducted is now published in reputed economics journals and is widely cited (see Google Scholar). After finishing my Ph.D., I worked as a Product Owner at Cognigy GmbH, a leader in the emerging Conversational AI industry as per the latest Gartner Magic Quadrant for Enterprise Conversational AI Platforms.
