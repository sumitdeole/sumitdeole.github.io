I am a Research Assistant at the faculty of Business Administration at Trier University (Germany), where I work with Prof. Dr. Marc Oliver Rieger. I graduated with a B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from VJTI, Mumbai (India) in 2008. After that, I began my journey in the beautiful world of data science, focusing on applied economics and causal inference. For this, I moved to France and completed my master's education from reputed economics schools in France, e.g., Toulouse and Marseille.

I moved to Germany for my doctoral education. I now have a Ph.D. degree in Economics from the University of Halle-Wittenberg (Germany), where I was advised by Prof. Dr. Wolf-Heimo Grieben. During my doctoral studies, I was introduced to the state-of-the-art data science methods of causal inference. The research subsequently conducted is now published in reputed economics journals and is widely cited (See Google Scholar).

After finishing my Ph.D., I worked as a Product Owner at Cognigy GmbH, a leader in the emerging Conversational AI industry as per the latest Gartner Magic Quadrant for Enterprise Conversational AI Platforms. I have been fortunate to work with a fantastic set of industry experts and researchers in the past.
